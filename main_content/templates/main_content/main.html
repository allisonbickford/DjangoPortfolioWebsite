School History

I started off as a pre-med student at University of South Florida. I wanted to go to school away from home and experience a new environment. After a year in Florida I realized medical school wasn't for me and took a couple classes in programming. I ended up falling in love with it and decided a change of major was a good opportunity to go to school back near my hometown in Michigan for cheaper tuition. Since then I've been really enjoying my computer science classes and can't wait to finish my degree!

Work History

Although programming is my favorite kind of work, I have also been working in the restaurant industry for four and a half years years to help pay for school. I started off at Russ' Restaurants and performed various jobs including prep, hosting, and serving for a total of four years. My most recent job is serving at Red Robin, where I have been since August 2018. There I take orders, deliver food, process payment, and cater to customers' needs in general.

My Goal

My current goal is to obtain a full-time internship during the summer that's related to software development. I enjoy front-end development (like you see here).
